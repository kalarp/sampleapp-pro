Frequently Asked Questions

What databases does SampleApp Pro support?

SampleApp Pro supports multiple databases including PostgreSQL, MongoDB, MySQL, Oracle, and SQLite. We continuously expand our database connector ecosystem.

How secure is SampleApp Pro?

We implement industry-standard encryption, support multi-factor authentication, and provide comprehensive audit logging for all data transformations.

Can I customize transformation rules?

Yes! SampleApp Pro offers extensive customization through Python-based transformation functions and a flexible plugin architecture.
