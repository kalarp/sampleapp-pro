Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SampleApp Pro. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you visit our project website. This project is for educational purposes only and data collected will not be shared with anyone.

We aim to follow best practices in data privacy, including principles from the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679, even though this project is not for commercial use.

2. Data Controller Information

The data controller for this project is:

Piotr Kalarus
sampleapppro@gmail.com

If you have any questions about how your data is handled, please contact us at sampleapppro@gmail.com.

3. What Data We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Technical Data (e.g., IP address, browser type, device information)
  • Usage Data (e.g., pages visited, time spent on pages, interactions with the site)
  • Cookies & Tracking Data (see Section 6 on Cookies)

We collect this data using web analytics tools such as Google Analytics and Matomo for educational purposes only.

4. Why We Collect Your Data

We process your data solely for the purpose of:

  • Understanding website usage – To analyze how visitors interact with our project website for learning purposes.
  • Ensuring website functionality – To detect and fix technical issues.

The legal basis for processing your data is your consent, as this project is only shared with individuals who voluntarily access the page.

5. How We Share Your Data

This project does not share, sell, or rent your data to any third parties. However, we use third-party analytics tools, such as:

  • Hotjar – To analyze website traffic (data is anonymized where possible).
  • Matomo – To track visitor interactions within the scope of this project.
  • Plausible – To track visitor interactions within the scope of this project.
  • Google Analytics – To track visitor interactions within the scope of this project.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and tracking technologies to improve website functionality and collect analytics data. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time.

The following cookies may be used on this website:

  • Essential Cookies
    • Purpose: Required for website functionality
    • Duration: Session
    • These cookies cannot be disabled
  • Analytics Cookies
    • Purpose: Used for tracking website usage statistics
    • Cookie name: trackingConsent
    • Duration: 1 year
    • Provider: SampleApp Pro
    • These cookies require your explicit consent
  • Third-party Analytics Cookies (example only - no tracking yet)
    • Google Analytics (_ga, _gid, _gat)
    • Purpose: Track user behavior on the website
    • Duration: Varies (2 years for _ga)
    • Provider: Google LLC
    • These cookies are only set if you accept analytics cookies

You can control your cookie preferences by clicking the buttons in our cookie consent banner. You can also manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

If you choose to decline cookies, certain parts of the website may not function as expected.

7. Your Rights

Since this project follows best practices in data privacy, you have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent at any time – If you no longer want your data to be tracked, you can contact us to request data removal.
  • Manage cookies – Adjust your browser settings to block cookies.
  • Access your data – If you want to know what data has been collected, you can request this information.

To exercise any of these rights, please email sampleapppro@gmail.com.

8. Data Retention

Since this is a temporary school project, we will retain collected data only for the duration of the project. After the project is completed, all data will be deleted within 3 months.

9. How We Protect Your Data

We take reasonable measures to protect the collected data from unauthorized access. However, since this is a small-scale educational project, security measures are limited.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Since this is a temporary project, changes to this policy are unlikely. However, if we make updates, they will be posted here.

11. Contact Us

If you have any concerns or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

sampleapppro@gmail.com
